Greatest Basketball Players of all time

Here is a non exhaustive list of the three greatest basketball players that have played in the NBA

Start now

Michael Jordan

Jordan played in the NBA in the 80's, 90's and early 2000's. He became the face of the league and dominated his era. He played fifteen seasons in the National Basketball Association, winning six NBA championships with the Chicago Bulls. He was an accessible star who managed to maintain an air of mystique. He is also known for "Air Jordan" his shoe brand with Nike that everyone can recognize the logo.

Kobe Bryant

A shooting guard, he spent his entire 20-year career with the Los Angeles Lakers in the National Basketball Association. He broke many records and affirmed his mamba mentality. He tragically passed away in 2018 leaving a huge legacy behing him.

Lebron James

Last but not least, Lebron James, aka the King. Standing at 6 foot 9, his strenght and speed are unbelievable. He is the only one on this list that is still currently playing. Drafted in 2003, and now at the age of 37 years old, he is still dominating the league, it seems like age does not affect him.
